By clinicians, for clinicians

We are a mix of clinicians, developers, and clinician-developers who were fed up with the state of technology in healthcare.

The push towards implementing electronic health records has led to a system where a single shift can average 4,000 clicks. This has led to technology built "for billing" rather than "for health," and it's causing an epidemic of burnout among providers.

Thankfully, the technology landscape has changed. We now have the ability to take these designs into our own hands.

Why MayJuun?
